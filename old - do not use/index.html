About Me

Full Stack Developer!

At a young age I knew I wanted to be a software developer. I recieved my first computer and ws instantly hooked on how to implement my own ideas and applications.

During my time at the University of Mississippi I studied to be a software developer. That went well for a bit, but regretfully I soon had to withdraw from the program for personal reasons.

When I did resume my higher education I chose a different path. I became a Draftsman. I spent many years drawing the plans to metal buildings and detailing all the parts and pieces needed for building erection. This decision was goood for a while, but I still did some software development in the form of designing websites.

One day a friend shared a post on a social media site advertising Base Camp Coding Academy Graduate Institute. This immediately caught my interest. I saw this as a second chance to properly do what I want to do from the begining. I applied for enrollment, persuaded a person to nominate me and then I was accepted.

After starting Base Camp I have recieved an abundance of training in different programing languages and Project Management. The journey so far has been amazing. I have even earned certifications in programing languages not taught at Base Camp. I have met lots of new people I am proud to call friends. More like a second family. Hopefully we will stay in contact for the forseable future.

So, Kelvin Owens isn't just a designer of buildings anymore. He's a designer of digital worlds, a master of logic and magic, a code-wielding adventurer who's always learning and growing. And who knows what he'll build next. Maybe a website that talks to dogs, or a game where you fly through space on a firebreathing dragon! The possibilities are endless, because Kelvin Owens, the developer, is just getting started!
