About Me

Early Tech Spark:

From a young age, I knew software development was my calling. My first computer ignited a passion for turning ideas into applications.

University Detour & Drafting Expertise:

The University of Mississippi welcomed me as a software development student. While initially fulfilling, personal circumstances necessitated my withdrawal from the program.

Drafting & Web Design: A Creative Bridge:

Seeking stability, I pursued a new path: draftsmanship. For years, I meticulously detailed metal building plans, ensuring each component's accuracy for construction. Though fulfilling, the developer spark within me remained, finding expression in website design.

Second Chance at Base Camp:

A social media post brought Base Camp Coding Academy Graduate Institute into my sights. Recognizing it as my chance to finally pursue my dream, I applied, secured a nomination, and earned a coveted spot.

Thriving at Base Camp:

Base Camp has provided extensive training in diverse programming languages and project management. The journey has been exhilarating, leading me to certifications in languages beyond their curriculum. I've also forged close bonds with incredible people, not just classmates, but a true second family. I cherish the hope of remaining connected for the long term.

Beyond Blueprints: Building Digital Worlds:

Kelvin Owens is no longer confined to blueprints. I'm a designer of digital worlds, a code-wielding adventurer perpetually honing my skills. The possibilities are boundless, whether it's crafting a dog-whispering website or soaring through space on a fire-breathing dragon. My developer journey has just begun!
