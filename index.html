About Me

Full Stack Developer!

Kelvin wasn't always staring at screens and typing like a machine. He used to spend his days with rulers and drawing boards, building buildings on paper, not with bricks! He was a super precise CAD draftsman, making sure every line and angle was perfect before the builders got to work.

But deep down, there was a different bug living in Kelvin's brain. He loved the way things worked, the logic that made them tick. So, he took a giant leap and jumped into Base Camp Coding Academy, a place where words turned into magic on screens.

First, he learned Python, kind of like his building blocks of logic. Every word was a brick, and he could build all sorts of cool things, like solving puzzles and making machines work. It was like learning a new language, but for computers!

Then, HTML and CSS became his paintbrushes. He could build castles on screens, not with bricks and mortar, but with colorful lines and fancy squares. He made buttons you could click, pictures that popped up, and websites that looked awesome.

But the story doesn't end there! Kelvin is still learning new coding languages, like a superhero who keeps adding tools to his belt. He's got a special sidekick called Git, who helps him keep track of all his building blocks and make sure nothing gets lost.

So, Kelvin Owens isn't just a builder of buildings anymore. He's a builder of digital worlds, a master of logic and magic, a code-wielding adventurer who's always learning and growing. And who knows what he'll build next? Maybe a website that talks to dogs, or a game where you fly through space on a rainbow unicorn! The possibilities are endless, because Kelvin Owens, the developer, is just getting started!
